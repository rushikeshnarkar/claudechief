# How to Use Claude for Sales Outreach Automation

Sales outreach is a numbers game — until it's not. The founders who scaled to $10M ARR didn't do it by sending 10x more emails. They did it by being smarter about every touchpoint. Claude skills are making that kind of intelligence available to every sales team, at scale.

## The Problem with Manual Outreach

Most sales teams are stuck in a manual loop:
- Hours spent researching each prospect
- Generic emails that nobody replies to
- Inconsistent follow-up sequences
- CRM updates that nobody does

The result: low reply rates, missed opportunities, and reps burning out.

## Claude-Powered Sales Stack

### Cold Email Writer Pro

This is the foundation of any sales Claude stack. Give it a prospect's name, company, role, and pain point — it generates a personalized cold email that actually sounds human. Not generic. Not AI-slop. Warm, specific, and relevant.

**What makes it different:**
- Researches the prospect's context
- Aligns the message with their specific pain
- Includes social proof naturally
- Has a clear, low-friction CTA

### LinkedIn Message Generator

LinkedIn outreach is a different format. This skill adapts your pitch for short-form, connection-based communication — with the right tone for each industry and persona.

### Meeting Brief Generator

Before a sales call, you need to know everything. This skill generates a one-page meeting brief from a prospect's LinkedIn, company website, and recent news — so you walk in prepared.

### Follow-Up Sequence Writer

The fortune is in the follow-up. This skill generates a 5-touch follow-up sequence that stays human and warm, not robotic and desperate. Different messages for different stages: no-reply, opened-but-no-reply, replied-but-no-meeting.

### CRM Update Assistant

Reps hate logging notes. This skill transcribes your call notes and updates your CRM fields automatically — saving 15 minutes per call and making your pipeline data actually useful.

## Building Your Outreach Pipeline

The best sales teams aren't just using individual skills — they're chaining them. A typical Claude-powered outreach workflow:

1. **Research** — Paste a prospect's URL, get a one-page brief
2. **Write** — Generate a personalized cold email in 30 seconds
3. **Sequence** — Build a 5-touch follow-up sequence
4. **Log** — Push everything to your CRM automatically
