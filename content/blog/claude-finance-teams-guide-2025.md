# Claude for Finance Teams: Budgeting, Forecasting, and Reporting

Finance teams are buried in work. Month-end close. Board decks. Budget reviews. Variance analysis. The strategic work that actually moves the business forward gets pushed to the bottom of the queue because the operational work never ends. Claude is changing that math.

## The Finance Workload Problem

Modern finance teams are doing more with less:
- More data sources to consolidate
- More frequent reporting cycles
- More stakeholders demanding insights
- Narrower windows to deliver

The result: brilliant finance professionals spending 60% of their time on work that should take 10%.

## Claude Skills for Finance

### 1. Monthly Financial Report Generator

This skill takes your raw numbers — revenue, costs, KPIs — and transforms them into a board-ready narrative. Not just "what happened" but "what it means" and "what to do about it."

**Output includes:**
- Executive summary (2-3 paragraphs)
- Key variance analysis
- Traffic light risk assessment
- Forward-looking commentary

### 2. Budget Variance Analyzer

Upload your budget vs. actuals and get an instant breakdown: which line items are off, by how much, and why. No more spending 4 hours in Excel to find what Claude finds in 30 seconds.

### 3. Forecast Model Builder

Building a rolling forecast is time-consuming. This workflow takes your historical data and existing assumptions, then generates a structured forecast model with scenario analysis built in.

### 4. KPI Dashboard Narrator

You've built a dashboard. Now you need to explain it to the CEO. This skill reads your KPI data and generates plain-language commentary: what moved, what it means, and what to ask about.

### 5. Accounts Payable Categorizer

Every invoice needs to be coded correctly. This skill categorizes incoming invoices against your chart of accounts, flags exceptions, and generates batch upload files for your ERP.

### 6. Audit Preparation Assistant

Audit season is stressful. This skill organizes your supporting documentation, generates trial balance summaries, and creates the narratives auditors need — reducing prep time by 70%.

We know — finance data is sensitive. All Claude skills run on your prompts, not your raw data files. For sensitive documents, use Claude's built-in data security features and never paste actual account numbers or SSNs into prompts.
