# Claude Operations: Automate Your Business Operations from Scratch

Operations is the backbone of every company. HR, compliance, vendor management, process documentation, vendor contracts, onboarding — if it's not a product feature or a sales call, it probably lands on the ops team's desk.

And yet, ops teams are the most undertooled. They spend hours on tasks that should take minutes. Claude is changing that.

## The Operations Overhead Problem

Ops work has a unique challenge: it's varied, unpredictable, and never-ending. You're managing:
- Recurring processes (onboarding, offboarding, reviews)
- One-off crises (vendor issues, compliance questions)
- Documentation that nobody wants to write
- Communication that nobody has time for

The result: ops teams are constantly reactive, never strategic.

## Claude Skills for Operations

### 1. Employee Onboarding Workflow

Onboarding is the first impression a new hire has of your company. This workflow generates a complete onboarding plan: week 1 schedule, required setup, key meetings, documentation access, first 30/60/90 day milestones. Ready in minutes, personalized to the role.

### 2. Process Documentation Generator

"Document our process" is the task that never gets done because it's tedious. This workflow takes your verbal process description and converts it into professional documentation: steps, ownership, exceptions, tools, and success criteria.

### 3. Vendor Evaluation Matrix

Evaluating vendors is a multi-dimensional problem: price, quality, reliability, support, contract terms. This workflow generates a structured evaluation matrix, scores vendors across your criteria, and recommends the best fit.

### 4. SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) Builder

Every repeatable process needs an SOP. This workflow takes any repeatable ops task and builds a complete SOP: purpose, scope, steps, roles, escalation points, and measurement.

### 5. Compliance Checklist Generator

Compliance requirements change constantly. This workflow generates a compliance checklist for your specific regulation (SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, etc.) and your company size — so you know exactly what's due and when.

### 6. Meeting Facilitator Brief

Running an ops meeting? This workflow generates a facilitator brief from your agenda: pre-work to share, discussion questions, decision frameworks, and post-meeting action items.

### 7. Offboarding Checklist

When an employee leaves, there's a long list of things to do. This workflow generates a complete offboarding checklist: IT revocation, asset return, knowledge transfer, final payroll, and compliance steps.

## Building an Ops Playbook with Claude

The most effective ops teams aren't just using individual skills — they're building complete playbooks. A modern ops playbook built with Claude:

1. **Onboarding** → Generate personalized onboarding plans for each role
2. **Processes** → Document every repeatable process as an SOP
3. **Compliance** → Track requirements with automated checklists
4. **Offboarding** → Smooth, complete offboarding every time
5. **Vendors** → Evaluate and manage vendors with structured frameworks

Everything documented. Everything systematized. Everything improved.
